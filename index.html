Be curious, not judgmental.

Ted Lasso is a gift. On the surface, it’s the story of an American football coach (and his sidekick) who moves to London to coach soccer… errr… football. The show is delightful in so many ways. It is charming, witty, silly, hysterical… and on the other it is sad, beautiful, thoughtful, honest, and kind. It’s a lot. It’s like being a human. We all need a little Ted right now.
